Midazolam

Strong healing drug that temporarily stuns user when used.

Midazolam is a common medicine composed of tonic liquid, ethanol and chloral hydrate. It is found and produced in Nexpharma facilities.
It's very powerful at healing damage and minor burns, but deals slight alcohol intoxication and makes the user unconcious for a few seconds.

If you have sufficient medical skill, during a duration of 3 seconds you will have these amounts applied per second:
  • -20 for afflictions of the type damage.
  • -3.2 for afflictions of the type burn.
  • +20 for affliction of the identifier drunk.
  • +1.5 for affliction of the identifier stun.
The total values for when you have unsufficient medical skill are the same, but it's applied over 6 seconds.
As such, math dictates that their values per second must then be half to keep the total the same.
Example: Given nt = 60, if t = 3, then n = 20, but if t = 6, then n = 10.
