Opium

A basic healing chemical that can treat internal damage.

Opium is a chemical that is used in the production of other medicine. It's produced by Nexpharma and can be found inside their facilities.
It's good at healing damage and minor burns, but it will lower your oxygen levels. Taking too much will lead to addication and eventual overdose.

If you have a sufficent enough medical skill of 16, during a duration of 5 seconds you will have these effects applied per second:
  • -3 for afflictions of the type damage.
  • -1 for afflictions of the type burn.
  • +3.75 for afflictions of the type oxygenlow.
  • +1 for afflictions of the type opiateaddiction.
  • +1.76 for afflictions of the type opiateoverdose.
  • -3 for afflictions of the type opiatewithdrawl.

If you have a insufficent amount of medical skill (lower than 16), the total amount of the afflictions will be given out over a period of 10 seconds.
Example: Given (t*2)(a/2), if (5)(3) or 5 seconds of -3 damage, then it will be (10)(1.5) or 10 seconds of -1.5 damage.
